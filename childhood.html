About My Childhood

My name is Jesee Maina, and I was born and raised in Nyeri County. I have two loving parents, Charles and Eunice, and three siblings. I am the youngest, with two sisters (Daisy and Emma) and one brother (James).

I grew up in a Christian household and attended the Anglican church. One of my favorite childhood memories was celebrating Christmas, my favorite holiday. During that time, our family would come together for joyous celebrations and traditions.

As a child, I enjoyed playing street football, and it became my favorite childhood game. The memories of those days still bring a smile to my face.
