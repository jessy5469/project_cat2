My Educational Journey

I started my education journey at Umoja Junior School when I was 4 years old, attending preschool. I continued my studies at Umoja Junior for 7 years, progressing from kindergarten to class 4.

At the next stage of my education, I transferred to Aberdare Academy, a private boarding school, where I completed classes 5 to 8. At the age of 13, I sat for the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) exam and scored 377 marks.

For my high school education, I joined St Mary's Boys High School, where I studied for four years. After completing my high school studies, I sat for the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) examination and achieved a grade of C+.

Continuing my academic journey, I joined Zetech University, where I am currently pursuing my undergraduate studies in Information Technology (IT).
